Cooling Credit

From a society that profits by consuming nature
to a society that profits by restoring it.

Not carbon accounting alone. Cooling Credit is a proposed thermal and natural-regeneration accounting framework that values measurable actions that reduce heat, restore water cycles, and restart the Earth’s natural cooling functions.

Cooling Credit / クーリングクレジット

自然を壊すほど儲かる社会から、
自然を戻すほど儲かる社会へ。

炭素会計だけではなく、熱会計へ。クーリングクレジットは、熱負荷を下げ、水循環を回復し、地球本来の自然冷却機能を再起動する行為に価値を与える制度構想です。

إلى مجتمع يربح من استعادتها.

Problem: Carbon Credits Alone Do Not Cool the Planet

Carbon accounting is important, but climate crisis is experienced as heat: hot asphalt, dry soil, weakened water cycles, declining forest evapotranspiration, marine heat, and rising cooling demand.

Global warming is felt as heat, not as numbers on a carbon ledger.

Solution: Cooling Credit

Water is the planet’s cooling medium. Cooling Credit is a framework for restoring water cycles, soil moisture, evapotranspiration, urban cooling, forest functions, and ocean circulation, then assigning value to measurable cooling contributions.

Human incentive ↓ Investment and implementation ↓ Measured cooling contribution ↓ Cooling Credit ↓ Natural cooling-function recovery

Key rule: A Cooling Credit is not a license to emit. A Cooling Credit is proof of cooling contribution.

解決策：Cooling Credit

水は地球の冷却媒体である。クーリングクレジットは、水循環、土壌水分、蒸散、都市冷却、森林機能、海洋循環を回復させ、その冷却貢献を測定し価値化するための制度です。

人間の動機 ↓ 投資と実装 ↓ 実測可能な冷却貢献 ↓ クーリングクレジット ↓ 自然冷却機能の回復

重要ルール：クーリングクレジットは排出の免罪符ではない。クーリングクレジットは冷却貢献の証明である。

الحل: أرصدة التبريد

الماء هو وسيط تبريد الكوكب. أرصدة التبريد هي إطار لاستعادة دورة الماء، ورطوبة التربة، والنتح، وتبريد المدن، ووظائف الغابات، ودوران المحيطات، ثم منح قيمة للمساهمات القابلة للقياس في التبريد.

حافز بشري ↓ استثمار وتنفيذ ↓ مساهمة تبريد قابلة للقياس ↓ أرصدة التبريد ↓ استعادة وظائف التبريد الطبيعية

قاعدة مهمة: أرصدة التبريد ليست ترخيصا للانبعاث، بل دليل على مساهمة تبريد.

Seasonal and Regional Cooling Strategy Simulation

This simulation compares which Cooling Credit strategy is most effective under different climate and regional conditions: high-humidity rainy regions, humid hot summer cities, dry hot regions, and humid tropical cities.

Key insight: Cooling Credit is not a one-size-fits-all mist-cooling model. It is a thermal accounting framework that changes evaluation methods according to local humidity, rainfall, soil moisture, solar exposure, and seasonal conditions.

Climate / Region TypeMost Effective ModelCooling ScoreImplication
High-humidity rainy region / RH 85%Rainwater + Humus + Soil Moisture3.95Rainy seasons can function as preparation periods for storing future cooling resources.
Humid hot summer city / RH 62%Dehumidification + Airflow4.46WBGT reduction can become an immediate heat-risk countermeasure.
Dry hot region / RH 28%Mist Evaporative Cooling6.84Dry hot regions have high compatibility with evaporative cooling.
Humid tropical city / RH 78%Rainwater + Humus + Soil Moisture4.97Soil- and water-cycle-based cooling models can also apply to humid tropical regions.

This simulation suggests that Cooling Credits should evaluate the best cooling action for each climate zone, rather than applying the same method everywhere.

FAQ

Q. Is Cooling Credit a replacement for Carbon Credit?No. Emissions reduction remains necessary. Cooling Credit adds the missing layer: measurable cooling contribution and natural cooling-function recovery.
Q. Is it a license to emit?No. A Cooling Credit is not a license to emit. It is proof of cooling contribution.
Q. How can it be measured?Air temperature, surface temperature, WBGT, cooling electricity demand, soil moisture, vegetation recovery, and water-cycle indicators can be measured with sensors and local monitoring.

FAQ

Q. カーボンクレジットの代替ですか？いいえ。排出削減は必要です。クーリングクレジットは、そこに実測可能な冷却貢献と自然冷却機能回復という不足していた層を加えるものです。
Q. 排出の免罪符になりませんか？なりません。クーリングクレジットは排出許可ではなく、冷却貢献の証明です。
Q. どう測定しますか？気温、地表温度、WBGT、冷房電力、土壌水分、植生回復、水循環指標などをセンサーや地域観測で測定できます。
