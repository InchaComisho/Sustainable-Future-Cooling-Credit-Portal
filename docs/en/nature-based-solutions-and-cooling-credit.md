# Nature-Based Solutions and Cooling Credits

Nature-based solutions use natural systems to solve social and environmental problems. Cooling Credits value natural cooling functions such as evapotranspiration, soil moisture, shade, water retention, carbon fixation, and ecosystem recovery.

[← Back to English Keyword Connection Map](KEYWORD_CONNECTION_MAP.md)

---

## What are nature-based solutions?

Nature-based solutions use forests, soils, water, wetlands, oceans, and ecosystems to solve climate, water, food, disaster-risk, and public-health challenges.

When nature's value remains abstract, implementation and investment are difficult to sustain.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits evaluate natural cooling functions as measurable outcomes and help institutionalize nature-based solutions.

```text
Nature-Based Solutions
↓
Evapotranspiration, water retention, shade, and ecosystem recovery
↓

```
