# Climate Adaptation and Cooling Credits

Climate adaptation means reducing damage from climate change already in progress. Cooling Credits connect climate adaptation to measurable heat-load reduction, water-cycle recovery, urban cooling, soil restoration, forest regeneration, and ocean-circulation support.

[← Back to English Keyword Connection Map](KEYWORD_CONNECTION_MAP.md)

---

## What is climate adaptation?

Climate adaptation protects societies, cities, agriculture, water resources, and ecosystems from the damage of climate change already underway.

If adaptation remains limited to disaster planning or equipment replacement, the underlying heat load remains in place.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits connect adaptation from responding to damage toward reducing the heat load itself.

```text
Climate Adaptation
↓
Heat illness, drought, heavy rain, wildfire, and urban heat response
↓
Measurable heat-load reduction
↓
Cooling Credits
```
