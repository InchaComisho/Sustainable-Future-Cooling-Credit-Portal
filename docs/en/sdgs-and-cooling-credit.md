## What are the SDGs?

The SDGs are global goals covering poverty, food, water, cities, climate, oceans, land ecosystems, and human well-being.

Many of these goals depend on stable water cycles, climate systems, soils, forests, and oceans.

If the Earth’s natural cooling functions continue to weaken, many SDG targets become harder to achieve.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits connect the SDGs to a shared physical foundation: the recovery of natural cooling functions.

```text
SDGs
↓
Water, food, cities, climate, oceans, and land
↓
Natural cooling-function recovery
↓
Cooling Credits
```

Urban cooling, soil restoration, forest regeneration, and ocean-circulation support can contribute to multiple SDG-related domains at once.
