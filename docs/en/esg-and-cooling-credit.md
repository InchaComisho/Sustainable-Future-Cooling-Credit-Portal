## What is ESG?

ESG refers to environmental, social, and governance factors used in investment and corporate evaluation.

However, ESG can remain abstract if it is not connected to measurable environmental outcomes.

Cooling Credits add a measurable layer: real-world heat-load reduction and natural cooling-function recovery.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits can help connect ESG to regional cooling, disaster-risk reduction, lower cooling demand, and natural regeneration.

```text
ESG
↓
Measurable environmental contribution
↓
Heat-load reduction
↓
Cooling Credits
```
