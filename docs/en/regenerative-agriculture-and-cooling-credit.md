# Regenerative Agriculture and Cooling Credits

Regenerative agriculture restores soil and ecosystem functions. Cooling Credits evaluate soil moisture, humus production, organic-matter circulation, microbial recovery, and reduced crop heat stress.

[← Back to English Keyword Connection Map](KEYWORD_CONNECTION_MAP.md)

---

## What is regenerative agriculture?

Regenerative agriculture restores soil organic matter, microbial activity, biodiversity, and water-holding capacity so farmland functions as a living ecosystem.

When farmland dries and stores heat, crop heat stress and water scarcity become more severe.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits connect regenerative agriculture to soil cooling and water-cycle recovery, not only food production.
