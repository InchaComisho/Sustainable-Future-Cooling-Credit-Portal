# Circular Economy and Cooling Credits

A circular economy keeps resources circulating instead of becoming waste. Cooling Credits connect food loss and organic waste to humus production, soil moisture recovery, microbial activity, carbon fixation, and surface cooling.

[← Back to English Keyword Connection Map](KEYWORD_CONNECTION_MAP.md)

---

## What is a circular economy?

A circular economy reuses, regenerates, and circulates resources rather than discarding them after a single use.

If organic matter is treated only as waste, its role in restoring soil and water cycles is lost.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits connect circular economy actions to soil moisture, microbial recovery, carbon fixation, and surface cooling.
