# Green Infrastructure and Cooling Credits

Green infrastructure uses natural functions as social infrastructure. Cooling Credits measure and value the cooling effects of greenery, rainwater, soil, forests, waterfronts, and ocean circulation.

[← Back to English Keyword Connection Map](KEYWORD_CONNECTION_MAP.md)

---

## What is green infrastructure?

Green infrastructure uses greenery, water, soil, rainwater, forests, and ecosystems as practical infrastructure for cities and regions.

When it is treated only as landscape improvement, its cooling and water-cycle functions are undervalued.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits measure green infrastructure as cooling infrastructure and turn its effects into investable value.
