# Ocean Restoration and Cooling Credits

Ocean restoration supports ocean circulation, oxygen supply, seaweed beds, fisheries, and plankton functions. Cooling Credits evaluate ocean-circulation support, OBS, OTU, fishery recovery, and marine cooling effects.

---

## What is ocean restoration?

Ocean restoration recovers ocean circulation, oxygen supply, seaweed beds, fisheries, plankton functions, and marine ecosystem stability.

If oceans continue storing excess heat, marine heat waves, oxygen depletion, fishery decline, and climate instability intensify.
