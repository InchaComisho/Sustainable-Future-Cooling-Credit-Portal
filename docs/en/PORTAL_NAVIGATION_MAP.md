# Cooling Credit Portal Navigation Map

This document defines the navigation structure of the **Sustainable Future Cooling Credit Portal**.

The portal is designed as the public-facing gateway to the Cooling Credit knowledge system.

---

## 1. Portal Role

The role of this portal is to connect common search terms and public-interest concepts to the Cooling Credit framework.

Search terms include:

- sustainability;
- sustainable future;
- SDGs;
- ESG;
- climate adaptation;
- climate resilience;
- green infrastructure;
- circular economy;
- water-cycle restoration;
- urban cooling;
- regenerative agriculture;
- ocean restoration;
- Civilization OS;
- Natural Complementary Science;
- Direct Planetary Cooling.

These terms are often discussed separately. The portal connects them to one operational pathway:

```text
Sustainable future
↓
Natural regeneration and water-cycle restoration
↓
Direct Planetary Cooling
↓
Cooling Credits
↓
Measurable cooling actions, MRV, finance, and implementation
```

## 5. What This Portal Is Not

This portal is not:

- a carbon offset marketplace;
- an adopted public standard;
- a securities offering;
- an emissions-permission system;
- a claim that cooling alone replaces emission reduction.

It is a knowledge gateway for connecting sustainability concepts to measurable physical cooling, natural cooling-function restoration, MRV, and implementation finance.

---

## 6. Summary

The portal exists to make one relationship clear:

```text
A sustainable future requires not only emission reduction,
but also the restoration of Earth’s physical cooling functions.
```

Cooling Credits are proposed as a way to make those cooling actions visible, measurable, fundable, maintainable, and governable.
