## What is Civilization OS?

Civilization OS is a design philosophy for sustaining civilization.

It treats cities, food, water, energy, forests, oceans, soils, AI, institutions, and markets as interconnected systems rather than separate sectors.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits function as an implementation layer for climate, water, soil, forest, ocean, and urban systems within Civilization OS.

A civilization cannot remain stable if it continuously accumulates heat and weakens natural cooling functions.

```text
Civilization OS
↓
Direct Planetary Cooling
↓
Natural cooling-function recovery
↓
Cooling Credits
↓
Social implementation and marketization
```

---

## Why Civilization OS needs Cooling Credits

Civilization depends on heat balance, water cycles, soils, forests, and oceans.

Cooling Credits connect natural regeneration to profit, investment, business development, and institutional implementation.

They help turn Civilization OS from a design concept into measurable climate-adaptation infrastructure.
