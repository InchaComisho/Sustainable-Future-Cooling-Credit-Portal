## What is urban cooling?

Urban cooling refers to actions that reduce heat islands, surface heat storage, building waste heat, air-conditioning exhaust, pedestrian heat stress, and overheating in public spaces.

If cities continue to accumulate heat, risks increase: heat illness, cooling electricity demand, nighttime heat, heavy-rain instability, and declining urban livability.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits turn urban cooling into measurable value.

```text
Urban Cooling
↓
Lower surface temperature
↓
Lower WBGT
↓
Lower cooling demand
↓
Cooling Credits
```
