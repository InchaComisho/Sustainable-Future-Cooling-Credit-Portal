# Water-Cycle Restoration and Cooling Credits

Water-cycle restoration means restoring rainwater, groundwater, soil moisture, evaporation, evapotranspiration, cloud processes, and rainfall cycles. Cooling Credits connect water-cycle recovery to direct planetary cooling and regional cooling.

[← Back to English Keyword Connection Map](KEYWORD_CONNECTION_MAP.md)

---

## What is water-cycle restoration?

Water-cycle restoration rebuilds the flow of rainwater, groundwater, soil moisture, evaporation, evapotranspiration, cloud processes, and rainfall.

When water disappears from surfaces and soils, natural cooling functions weaken across cities, farms, forests, and oceans.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits connect water-cycle recovery to measurable regional cooling and direct planetary cooling.

```text
Water-Cycle Restoration
↓
Rainwater, groundwater, soil moisture, and evapotranspiration recovery
↓
Regional cooling and natural cooling-function recovery
↓
Cooling Credits
```
