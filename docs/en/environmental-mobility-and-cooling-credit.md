# Environmental Mobility and Cooling Credits

[← Back to English Keyword Connection Map](KEYWORD_CONNECTION_MAP.md)

---

## What is environmental mobility?

Environmental mobility means transforming transport and movement systems to reduce environmental burden.

It often focuses on electric vehicles, public transport, low-carbon mobility, shared transport, and renewable energy.

However, mobility infrastructure also stores and emits heat.

Stations, bus stops, roads, parking areas, vehicles, and public spaces can intensify urban heat.

---

## Connection to Cooling Credits

Cooling Credits expand environmental mobility from decarbonization to cooling infrastructure.

```text
Environmental Mobility
↓
Cooling transport infrastructure
↓
Lower urban heat load
↓
Cooling Credits
```

Transport systems can be evaluated not only by emissions, but also by whether they reduce heat stress and cooling demand.
